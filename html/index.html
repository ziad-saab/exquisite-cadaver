EXQUISITE
CADAVER

accidental stories

The exquisite cadaver, also known as exquisite corpse or rotating corpse, is a game in which a story is collectively written. This method of storytelling comes in many flavours, and can even be applied to drawing (try it at home!). In our version, each contributor is only allowed to see what the previous contributor has written. The game, whose name comes from the French "cadavre exquis", was popularized by the French Surrealist movement.

  • Proper spelling and grammar
